Tamil Pillai app

This app is used to read a few freely available devotional poems in Tamil language. Poems are in-built. So, Internet is not required to read them.

Notable poems

Top poems available with this app:

  1. தேசிய கீதம்
  2. தமிழ்த்தாய் வாழ்த்து
  3. அபிராமி அந்தாதி
  4. ஹரிவராசனம்
  5. இறைவனிடம் கையேந்துங்கள்
  6. தேன் இனிமையிலும் இயேசுவின் நாமம்
  7. திருப்பாவை
  8. திருவெம்பாவை

This aoo is dedidated to my school teacher Vsaantha madam and also the Trust and the correspondent of the Siddhartha Matriculation School, where I studied, those days, like Late Dr. Jeevanantham, and Bharathi madam, who constantly encouraged Unity in Diversity through Music.
